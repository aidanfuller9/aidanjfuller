How can we use cycling to unite people and combat global climate issues?

Over the years, riding bikes has grown into a worldwide sport and has brought countless people together.

However, there are still underlying problems in the general diversity and pressing environmental issues such as climate change that we can ride bikes to combat.

  • Large and welcoming community
  • Great exercise, beneficial to mental health
  • Environmentally friendly
  1. Educate people on societal issues
  2. Teach them how riding bikes helps us combat these issues
  3. Get them on two wheels!
